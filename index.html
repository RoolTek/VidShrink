VidShrink Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 January 2026

VidShrink is a video compression application developed by RoolTek. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at: 7001tek@gmail.com

Summary

  • Videos are processed locally on your device. VidShrink does not upload your videos to any server.
  • The app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad services.
  • VidShrink only accesses files and folders you explicitly choose.

Video Processing

VidShrink compresses videos directly on your device. The app reads the video file you select, creates a compressed copy, and saves it to a location you choose. Video files are not uploaded or stored on external servers.

Permissions

Media access / storage permissions
Used only to allow you to select videos and choose where compressed files are saved. VidShrink does not access files without your action.

Notifications

Notifications may be used to show compression progress or completion. You can disable notifications at any time in system or app settings.

Advertising

VidShrink may show advertisements from third-party providers such as Google AdMob. These services may collect information such as device identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, and ad performance data in order to display and measure ads.

Data collection by advertising providers depends on your device settings and the provider’s own privacy policy.

Data Collected

VidShrink does not require user accounts and does not collect video content. Depending on enabled features and third-party SDKs, limited data may be collected:

  • Device and app information (Android version, device model)
  • Basic usage data (screen views, ad interactions)
  • Crash or diagnostic data (if crash reporting is enabled)

Data Sharing

Limited data may be shared with advertising providers strictly for ad delivery and measurement. VidShrink does not sell personal data.

Data Retention

VidShrink does not store video files on servers. Videos remain on your device. Third-party services may retain data according to their own policies.

Children’s Privacy

VidShrink is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you believe a child’s data has been collected through third-party services, please contact us.

Security

VidShrink uses standard best practices and reputable third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications at any time.
  • You can control file access permissions in Android settings.
  • You may control ad personalization through device privacy settings.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
